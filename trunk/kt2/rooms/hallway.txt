Scene: Down the hallway we see a door the bathroom, a door to the kitchen, and a door to the basement. The basement door is sort of scary looking. It has a padlock on it and looks as though something scary may lie behind it.

If bathroom door then go to Bathroom.

If kitchen door then go to Kitchen.

If basement door but don't have blue key then "You try to pull the door open, but the lock won't give. I should also mention that the lock is blue, because that could be useful information."

If basement door and blue key then show message "The key fits, but the lock seems reluctant to give. Maybe you should try it again."

If basement door and blue key is in inventory then go to Basement.

If the player clicks on the house take them to Entrance.
